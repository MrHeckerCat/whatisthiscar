F.A.Q.

What types of images does the tool support?

The tool supports a wide range of image formats, including JPEG, PNG, and BMP.

How accurate is the Car Recognition Tool?

The accuracy of the tool depends on various factors like image quality, lighting conditions, and the angle of the car. However, under optimal conditions, the tool can achieve an accuracy rate of over 90%.

Can you recognize any car model?

Our system is capable of recognizing a wide variety of car models with high accuracy.

Are uploaded images stored on your servers?

No, we do not store any images that you upload for car recognition. Uploaded images are temporarily stored in a secure cloud storage service for processing and are automatically deleted within 24 hours.
